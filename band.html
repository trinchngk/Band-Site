Solitaire

Solitaire is an indie cover band that captures the essence of dreamy, atmospheric, and soulful sounds from iconic indie artists like The Marias, Men I Trust, and Clairo. Formed out of a shared love for mellow grooves, lo-fi beats, and introspective lyrics, we bring fresh interpretations of your favorite indie hits. Known for our serene live performances and ethereal arrangements, Solitaire creates an immersive musical experience that transports audiences into a world of nostalgic vibes and modern indie elegance.
