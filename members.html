Members

Eva is the heart and soul of Solitaire's sound, blending her smooth, velvety voice with the band's laid-back instrumentals. Inspired by indie powerhouses like Clairo and Lana Del Rey, her voice resonates with a dreamy quality, perfect for the band's slow, melodic tunes. Eva grew up surrounded by music, having trained in classical voice before finding her passion for indie pop. Her on-stage presence is both captivating and effortless, drawing in listeners with every note.

Luke is the band's musical architect, weaving together intricate guitar melodies that shape Solitaire's signature sound. Influenced by the lush guitar work of Men I Trust and Tame Impala, he combines clean, minimalist riffs with atmospheric effects to create an otherworldly feel. Having played in several indie bands before joining Solitaire, Luke brings a depth of experience and musical creativity that drives the band's arrangements.

Dani is the backbone of Solitaire, holding down the groove with her warm, pulsing basslines. Her playing is inspired by bands like The Marias and Beach House, where the bass is smooth yet powerful, subtly guiding the music's flow. Offstage, Dani is known for her quiet demeanor, but once she picks up the bass, her dynamic energy takes center stage, adding depth to the band's sound.

Noah's drumming is the rhythmic heartbeat of Solitaire, providing the steady, chilled beats that the band is known for. Drawing influence from the electronic and lo-fi elements of artists like Clairo and Still Woozy, Noah incorporates soft percussion and electronic touches into the band's indie sound. His versatile style, from soft brush strokes to sharp snare hits, creates a fluid foundation for the band's mellow yet rhythmic tunes.
